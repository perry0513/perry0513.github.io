Profile

My name is Pei-Wei Chen. I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences working with Sanjit A. Seshia at the University of California, Berkeley. I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering at National Taiwan University, advised by Jie-Hong Roland Jiang.

My PhD research mainly lies in formal methods, automated reasoning, and neuro-symbolic approaches for them. During my undergrad I focused on Electronic Design Automation (EDA) and automated reasononing.

Here is my CV and Google Scholar. You can reach me at pwchen@berkeley.edu.
